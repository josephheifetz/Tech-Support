Project Ideas

Overview

Since the very start of my interest in the field of Information Technology, I have always been very passionate about developing games. Which comes to my project idea of developing a ‘Virtual Reality game of Cricket’. This project will consist of using the recently released “Virtual Reality” technology and will redecorate the fundamentals of a very underrated game in the sense of virtual gaming. From a very young age I have been very fond of this sport and since then have been wanting to promote this sport all over the world. Now that the world is fast developing and that people’s opinions and ideas are of importance, this project can make a significant impact!

Motivation

My motivation for this project came from experiencing the ‘Virtual Reality’ technology first hand. I figured that this technology is “the future” in various categories including gaming. The gaming world was unable to develop any game that required the user to have physical involvement with their whole body, especially their eyes. Until now, where the user is required to interact with the game in the sense that they are in the game. Which of course brings out their true emotions and reactions. Apart from the technology side of my project, I figured that I would need to find an activity where I will be able to get a decent amount of user activity. Therefore, I chose my category of Sport and in the field of Cricket.

Description

The sport of Cricket is underestimated in the ‘fun’ sense of a physical activity. Being a sport that has been around since the 16th Century, I figured that it was about time there was some sort of justice served for the undervalued sport. Also, seeing that the “Virtual Reality” technology was an insight to the future, especially in the sense of gaming. I was immediately grasped by it and decided to develop an idea of a game of Cricket involving the technology. The game will be named “VR Cricket” and will coincidentally allow the user to experience the fundamentals of the sport through the eyes of real cricketers and what they experience on the field. The game will require a lot of time as it will be developed on a fairly new device and with very intense features. To allow the user to have the best experience, they game will be conducted in a way where the user can adjust the settings and difficulty to their skill. This game is also being developed in the sense of potentially becoming useful to professional Cricketing teams to help the professional sportsmen improve their skill in various aspects. The game will rely a lot on user interactions as if the user is not moving simultaneously the player in game wont move and vice versa for when the user is moving.

Tools and Technology

The game will be developed through Unreal Engine as it is the most efficient and easy to use software for such projects. Once the game is developed, the User will be required to purchase the Virtual Reality headset for visionary fundamentals and a VR controller for in-game features. As the game is sports categorized, it doesn’t necessarily require the need of sound, therefore headphones are not fully recommended. The use of Maya and ZBrush will b essential as these softwares will determine the overall presentation of the game.

Skills Required

For this project, skills involving the use of Java or C# for coding, the use of the software “tracking” for head and body movements, Graphic Design, Animation, Audio Design, User Studies, Psychology to understand the emotions of users, method of “Stitching” for a 360 degree image, Adobe’s video editing software, basics of colour correction and the concept of Drones for capturing unique angles and video footage for virtual reality are all vital for this project to be successful in development.

Outcome

If this project is to be successful, I believe that will be a big step for the Cricketing and Virtual Reality communities. From this game users from around the globe will be able to connect and share their experience of a game involving an underrated sport and the use of futuristic technology. This will also be a major morale boost for me and through it I will be able to plan future projects involving similar technology but with a different gaming logic.
